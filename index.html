Recurrent PPO/DQN Trading Agent on Raw OHLCV

I framed trading as a reinforcement-learning problem, built a Gym environment over raw OHLCV, integrated an LSTM policy with RecurrentPPO (SB3-Contrib), added a Crew-AI decision layer, and backtested end-to-end. My contributions span environment, policy, training, inference JSON, Crew-AI decisioning, and backtesting glue.

What I Did (Concise)

  • Env: Implemented gym.Env, rewards with costs/slippage, unit tests.
  • Policy & RL: LSTM encoder + DQN/PPO heads; integrated SB3 RecurrentPPO and Eval/Stop callbacks.
  • Training: Ran training on historical data; saved best checkpoints.
  • Inference: JSON output for { action_probs }; latency benchmark routine.
