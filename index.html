RDNN: Recurrent RL Trading Agent on Raw OHLCV

Project: a Gym-based trading environment using raw OHLCV windows and a recurrent RL policy (RecurrentPPO from SB3-Contrib) to choose SELL/HOLD/BUY. My contributions: built the gym.Env with costs/slippage, implemented the LSTM policy wrapper and RecurrentPPO wiring, ran training with evaluation/early-stopping, exposed an inference JSON ({"action_probs":[...]}) with a latency benchmark, integrated a Crew-AI DecisionAgent to map probabilities to BUY/SELL/HOLD, and created a Backtrader harness to turn model outputs into trades and metrics.
